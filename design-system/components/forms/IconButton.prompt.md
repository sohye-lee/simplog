Square icon-only button for row actions, toolbars, and the month stepper. Always pass a `label` for accessibility.

```jsx
<IconButton label="Previous month" variant="outline"><Icon name="chevron-left" /></IconButton>
<IconButton label="Delete row"><Icon name="trash-2" /></IconButton>
```

Variants: `ghost` (default), `outline`, `solid`. Sizes: `sm` (28), `md` (36), `lg` (44).
