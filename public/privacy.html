Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

SimplyLog is a personal expense tracker. We collect as little as possible and never sell your data.

What we collect

  • Your email address — used only to sign you in (a one-time code is emailed to you) and to sync your data across your devices.
  • The entries you create — amount, category, subcategory, note, and date for each expense or income you log, plus your category list and app settings (currency, recurring rules).
  • Anonymous usage analytics — via Google Analytics, we collect aggregated, non-identifying information about how the app is used (screens viewed, features used, device type, approximate region, and a random analytics identifier) to understand and improve SimplyLog. This is never linked to your entries or your identity, and IP addresses are anonymized. It is disabled if your browser sends a "Do Not Track" signal.

We do not collect your name, contacts, or precise location, we do not use advertising identifiers, we do not show ads, and we never sell your data or use it to track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Where your data lives

Your entries are stored on your device and, when you are signed in, synced to your private account so the same data appears on your phone, tablet, and the web. Only you can read your data — it is protected by row-level security tied to your account.

Service providers we use

  • Supabase — database and authentication (stores your account and synced entries).
  • Resend — sends the sign-in code emails.
  • Vercel — hosts the web app.
  • Google Analytics — anonymous, aggregated usage measurement.

These providers process data only to run SimplyLog on our behalf and under their own security and privacy commitments.

Your control over your data

  • Export — download everything anytime as CSV, Excel, PDF, or a full JSON backup from the app.
  • Erase — "Reset data" in Settings removes all your entries from your account.
  • Delete your account — Settings → Account → "Delete account" permanently erases your account and all associated data from the app. You can also email support@simplylog.app.

Data retention

We keep your data only while your account exists. When you delete your account (or all your entries), the data is removed from our systems.

Children

SimplyLog is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and, for material changes, note it in the app.

Contact

Questions or requests: support@simplylog.app
